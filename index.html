Welcome To Kingoo Cottages

Little Story

Nestled in the heart of Kapchorwa, Kingoo Cottages offers a tranquil retreat surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. Located near the stunning Sipi Falls and the lush landscapes of Mount Elgon National Park, our cottages provide the perfect escape for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and cultural explorers. Guests can embark on scenic hikes to Sipi Falls, explore caves, or take guided coffee tours to experience the rich aroma of Uganda’s finest Arabica coffee.

For wildlife enthusiasts, Mount Elgon National Park is home to diverse species, including elephants, buffaloes, primates, and over 300 bird species. The nearby Kapkwai Exploration Center serves as a gateway to thrilling trails leading to waterfalls, escarpments, and the world’s largest caldera. Visitors can also immerse themselves in the vibrant culture of the Sebei people, experiencing traditional dances, storytelling, and unique customs.

At Kingoo Cottages, we offer a serene and comfortable stay with breathtaking views, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and adventure. Whether you're here to hike Towei Hill, enjoy a peaceful bird-watching experience, or simply unwind in nature, we welcome you to discover the magic of Kapchorwa with us.
